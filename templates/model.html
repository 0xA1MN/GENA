What is the Radixact® System?

The Radixact System is a singular radiation therapy system that effectively treats cancer anywhere in the body with high precision. The only radiation therapy system designed on a CT scanner foundation, the Radixact System uses daily imaging to guide the treatment each day and deliver highly accurate radiation dose from 360 degrees around the patient.

A distinctive feature of this system is the new generation of spiral radiation therapy. In addition, the device combines a linear gas pedal and a computed tomograph for precise visualization of the tumor.

Double multi-blade collimators located on different sides are capable of conducting a comprehensive 360-degree irradiation of the target, directing the radiation precisely within the specified limits and adjusting the radiation dose if necessary.

Tomotherapy treatment

It is a non-invasive procedure that destroys cancer cells to stop their growth and is one of the most effective treatments for cancer.

Before the process begins, the radiation doses are carefully calculated and its technique is determined in order to destroy tumor cells as much as possible while protecting healthy tissue.

The radiation oncologist determines the amount of radiation and selects the number of tomotherapy sessions required. Dosimetric calculations are performed by a team of highly qualified specialists. It may take several days to make a decision. This procedure is called radiation therapy planning.
